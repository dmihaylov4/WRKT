## üîí Privacy Policy - CRITICAL

**Status:** ‚ö†Ô∏è **YOU MUST UPDATE YOUR PRIVACY POLICY**

Since you now collect user data (accounts, social interactions, posts, etc.), you **must** have a compliant privacy policy.

### **What Your Privacy Policy Must Cover:**

1. **Account Information:**
   - Email, username, display name
   - How it's used (account creation, authentication)
   - How it's stored (Supabase cloud database)

2. **User-Generated Content:**
   - Workout posts, comments, photos
   - Who can see this data (friends, public, private)
   - How long it's retained

3. **Social Features:**
   - Friend connections
   - Battle/challenge participation
   - Activity feeds

4. **HealthKit Data:**
   - What health data is accessed
   - How it's used (display in app, sync workouts)
   - **IMPORTANT:** Explicitly state you don't share HealthKit data with third parties
   - How it's stored (locally on device, not uploaded to servers)

5. **Analytics & Tracking:**
   - What analytics you collect (if any)
   - Third-party services used (Firebase, etc.)

6. **Data Deletion:**
   - How users can delete their data
   - What happens when account is deleted

7. **Contact Information:**
   - Your email for privacy inquiries

### **Quick Solution:**

Use a privacy policy generator:
- **Termly** (https://termly.io/) - Free tier available
- **PrivacyPolicies.com** (https://www.privacypolicies.com/)
- **iubenda** (https://www.iubenda.com/)

Or hire a lawyer for a custom policy (recommended if you plan to scale).
