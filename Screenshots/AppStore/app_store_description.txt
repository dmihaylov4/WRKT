APP STORE DESCRIPTION - WRKT
================================

Transform your fitness journey with WRKT - the social fitness app that makes working out competitive, accountable, and fun.

TRAIN TOGETHER, PUSH HARDER

Challenge friends to head-to-head battles. Who can lift more volume this week? Who can hit the most PRs? Create custom battles or choose from dozens of competitive formats. Real-time scores keep you motivated to push harder.

Join community challenges with thousands of others - from "30-Day Warrior" to "100K Club" - and see exactly where you rank. Your friends don't let you skip leg day.

SOCIAL WORKOUT FEED

Share your workouts with photos and celebrate your progress. Like and comment on friends' posts. See what your crew is training and get inspired. Every rep, every PR, every milestone - share it all.

COMPETE WITH 22+ METRICS

Battle on total volume, workout consistency, specific exercises, cardio distance, calories burned, heart rate zones, and more. Filter by muscle groups, exercises, or intensity levels. Create the perfect challenge for any goal.

COMPREHENSIVE TRACKING

• 3,000+ exercises with video demonstrations
• Smart weight suggestions based on your history
• Automatic PR tracking for every exercise
• Interactive muscle group visualization with recovery status
• Training balance analytics (push/pull, muscle groups)
• Level system with XP progression
• Achievement badges and milestones

CALENDAR & STREAKS

• Beautiful workout calendar with weekly goals
• Streak tracking with freeze days
• Progress charts and training trends
• Weekly volume and consistency metrics

HEALTHKIT INTEGRATION

• Sync cardio workouts automatically
• Interactive route maps with heart rate overlay
• Heart rate zone analysis
• Track running, cycling, and more
• Import HealthKit strength workouts

SMART FEATURES

• Built-in rest timer with customizable presets
• Exercise library with filters and favorites
• Custom workout templates
• Muscle recovery visualization
• Training balance insights
• Weekly goal progress

FREE TO USE

WRKT is completely free with all features unlocked. No paywalls, no hidden costs, no premium tiers. Just download and start training.

JOIN THE COMMUNITY

Connect with training partners, challenge friends, join battles, and celebrate progress together. Whether you're a beginner or advanced lifter, WRKT gives you the tools and community to stay accountable.

Download WRKT today and never train alone again.
