## üéØ What Makes a Good Screenshot

### ‚úÖ Include:
- **Clear UI** - Interface is visible and readable
- **Real data** - Shows actual functionality (even if mock)
- **Social proof** - Posts, friends, battles show others using the app
- **Progression** - Show that users can track growth over time
- **Engagement** - Likes, comments, battle scores show active community

### ‚ùå Avoid:
- **Empty states** - "No data yet" screens
- **Settings screens** - Not exciting for first-time viewers
- **Duplicate concepts** - Don't show two battle screenshots
- **Too much text** - Let the UI speak for itself
