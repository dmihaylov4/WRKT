Privacy Policy for VOLIA

Last Updated: December 27, 2025
Effective Date: December 27, 2025

Introduction

VOLIA ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal information when you use our workout tracking application.

Key Points:

  • Workout tracking data is stored locally on your device
  • Social features use secure cloud storage (Supabase, EU region)
  • You control what data you share through privacy settings
  • We do not sell your personal information
  • We do not use third-party analytics or advertising services
  • Social features are entirely optional

Social Features and Data Sharing

IMPORTANT: VOLIA now includes optional social and competitive features that allow you to connect with friends, share workouts, and participate in challenges.

What's Stored in the Cloud

When you create an account and use social features, the following data is stored on Supabase cloud servers (EU region):

  • User Profile: Username, display name, email, bio, avatar photo
  • Workout Posts: Workouts you choose to share, captions, photos
  • Social Interactions: Comments, likes, post visibility settings
  • Friend Connections: Your friend list and friendship requests
  • Battles & Challenges: Competition participation, scores, leaderboards
  • Notifications: Real-time activity notifications

What Remains Local Only

The following data stays exclusively on your device:

  • Workouts you don't share to social feed
  • Exercise preferences and favorites
  • Rest timer settings
  • App preferences (units, haptics, etc.)

You're In Control

Social features are completely optional. You can:

  • Use VOLIA without creating an account (local-only mode)
  • Create an account but keep your profile private
  • Control who sees each workout post (public/friends/private)
  • Delete your social account and all cloud data at any time

Information We Collect

1. Workout Data

When you use VOLIA to track your workouts, we store the following information locally on your device:

  • Exercise names, sets, reps, and weights
  • Workout dates and duration
  • Personal records (PRs) and progress statistics
  • Custom exercise preferences and favorites
  • Rest timer settings

Purpose: To track your fitness progress and provide personalized workout recommendations.

2. HealthKit Data

With your explicit permission, VOLIA may access the following data from Apple Health:

  • Workout history
  • Heart rate data during workouts
  • Exercise time and calories burned
  • Cardio workout routes (running, cycling, walking)

Purpose: To provide comprehensive workout insights, sync workout data across your health apps, and display workout routes on maps.

Important: We only read this data to display it within the app. We save strength training workouts back to HealthKit so your fitness data stays synchronized across your devices.

3. Personal Information

  • Body weight: Used to calculate volume for bodyweight exercises (push-ups, pull-ups, etc.)
  • Preferences: Rest timer settings, weight units, haptic feedback preferences

4. Location Data (Optional)

If you grant location permission, we use your location data only to:

  • Display interactive route maps for cardio workouts synced from HealthKit
  • Show where you ran, cycled, or walked

Important: Location data is only used when the app is in use and only for displaying routes. We do not track your location in the background or share it with anyone.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  1. Track your workout progress - Store and display your exercise history
  2. Calculate statistics - Compute PRs, volume, training frequency, and muscle group balance
  3. Provide recommendations - Suggest appropriate weights based on your history
  4. Gamification - Award XP, levels, achievements, and rewards for motivation
  5. Sync with Health - Save strength training workouts to Apple Health
  6. Display routes - Show maps of your cardio activities from HealthKit

Data Storage and Security

Hybrid Storage Model

VOLIA uses a hybrid approach to data storage:

  • Local Storage: Workout tracking data is stored on your device
  • Cloud Storage: Social features use Supabase (EU region) for data sync and sharing
  • Your Choice: Social features are optional - use offline mode for local-only storage

Cloud Storage Security (Supabase)

When you use social features, your data is protected by:

  • End-to-end encryption - All data transmitted via HTTPS/TLS
  • EU data residency - Supabase servers located in European Union
  • Row-Level Security (RLS) - Database-level privacy enforcement
  • Authentication - Secure email/password authentication
  • Access controls - You control who sees your data (public/friends/private)

Local Data Protection

Data stored on your device is protected using:

  • File encryption - Data is encrypted when your device is locked (NSFileProtectionComplete)
  • iOS security - Leverages Apple's built-in device encryption
  • HealthKit privacy - All HealthKit access follows Apple's strict privacy guidelines
  • Sandboxed storage - App data isolated from other apps

Backups

Local data: Automatically backed up as part of your device's encrypted backup (iTunes/iCloud backup). These backups are controlled by Apple's backup policies and are encrypted.

Cloud data: Social feature data is stored on Supabase servers with automatic backups. You can export or delete this data at any time through the app.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to anyone.

What We Never Share

We do not share your data with:

  • Third-party analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Data brokers
  • Marketing partners
  • External social media platforms

Social Feature Sharing (Your Control)

When you use social features, you choose what to share:

  • Workout Posts - Visible based on your settings (public, friends-only, or private)
  • Profile Information - Username, display name, bio, avatar (visible to other users)
  • Battle/Challenge Data - Shared with battle/challenge participants
  • Comments & Likes - Visible to users who can see the post
  • Friend List - Visible only to you (not public)

Important: All social sharing is under your control via privacy settings and post visibility options.

Other Data Sharing

  • Apple Health - If you grant permission, we save workouts to HealthKit (under your control)
  • Export feature - You can manually export your data as CSV (completely optional)
  • Supabase - Social feature data stored on Supabase servers (see Third-Party Services section)

Social Feature Privacy Controls

VOLIA provides comprehensive privacy controls for social features. You have full control over what you share and who can see it.

Post Visibility Settings

For every workout post, you can choose:

  • Public: Visible to all VOLIA users (appears in public challenges, search)
  • Friends Only: Visible only to accepted friends
  • Private: Visible only to you (not shared)

You can change post visibility at any time, even after posting.

Profile Privacy

  • Private Profile: Make your entire profile private (only friends can see posts)
  • Friend Requests: Accept or decline friend requests
  • Block Users: Block users to prevent them from seeing your content or contacting you

Image Privacy

  • Workout photos are only visible based on post visibility settings
  • Avatar photos are visible to other users (public profile element)
  • All images stored in private Supabase storage buckets
  • You can delete images at any time

Friend-Only Data

The following data is never public and only visible to accepted friends:

  • Friend list (who you're connected with)
  • Detailed workout statistics
  • Real-time workout activity
  • Friend-only posts

Your Rights and Choices

Access Your Data

You can view all your data within the app at any time:

  • Workout history
  • Personal records
  • Statistics and progress
  • Achievements and rewards

Export Your Data

You can export your data at any time:

  • Workout data (CSV) - Settings → Developer Options → Export Data
  • Social data - Export posts, comments, friends list through app settings
  • Complete data package - Request full data export including all social interactions

Delete Your Data

You have complete control to delete your data:

Local Workout Data

  • Delete individual workouts - Swipe to delete in workout history
  • Clear all workouts - Settings → Developer Options → Clear all workout data
  • Delete the app - Uninstalling VOLIA permanently deletes all local data

Social Account & Cloud Data

  • Delete individual posts - Tap post → Delete (removes from cloud)
  • Remove friends - Unfriend users at any time
  • Delete social account - Settings → Account → Delete Account (permanent)
  • What gets deleted: Profile, posts, comments, likes, friend connections, battles/challenges
  • Timeline: Social data deletion is immediate and permanent

Important Notes

  • Deleting your social account does NOT delete local workout data
  • Workouts saved to Apple Health must be deleted separately through the Health app
  • Comments you left on friends' posts may remain visible (attributed to "Deleted User")

Manage HealthKit Permissions

You can revoke HealthKit permissions at any time:

  1. Open iOS Settings
  2. Go to Health → Data Access & Devices
  3. Select VOLIA
  4. Toggle off data types you want to revoke

Children's Privacy

VOLIA does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for users who can safely perform strength training exercises. If you believe a child has provided information through the app, please contact us.

HealthKit Specific Privacy

VOLIA uses Apple's HealthKit framework and adheres to Apple's strict privacy requirements:

  • Explicit consent required - We ask for your permission before accessing any HealthKit data
  • Granular permissions - You control exactly which data types we can access
  • Read and write transparency - We clearly explain what we read (workout history, heart rate) and write (strength workouts)
  • No HealthKit data sale - We will never sell or share HealthKit data with third parties
  • No marketing use - HealthKit data is never used for marketing or advertising purposes
  • Revocable access - You can revoke HealthKit permissions at any time through iOS Settings
